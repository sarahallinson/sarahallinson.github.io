...Tonnes of rubbish end up in the oceans, carried by currents into a giant swirling vortex. This is known as the Great Pacific Garbage Patch...
...Unbeknownst to humans, that rubbish has been slowly forming into a world-ending monster...
...I am a scientist from another planet. I have been sent here, to your "planet Earth", to help save your planet before it is too late... But I need your help!
Only you (a child in late primary school, early high school, or older) can save the day!

The Trash Kraken Rises

Face near-endless waves of rubbish in your quest to restore our environment! Collect and research the plastic polluting our water ways to discover and stop their sources before it can join the Kraken.

The more pollution you collect, the less the Trash Kraken can use to grow and take over the world. Use the collected rubbish to research where they come from and how to safely get rid of it.
