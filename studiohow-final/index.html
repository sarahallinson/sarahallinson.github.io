"The Trash Kraken Rises"

Game Concept

Face near-endless waves of rubbish in your quest to restore our environment! Collect and research the plastic polluting our water ways to discover and stop their sources before it can join the Kraken.

The more pollution you collect, the less the Trash Kraken can use to grow and take over the world. Use the collected rubbish to research where they come from and how to safely get rid of it.
