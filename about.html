About the book:
Integral_Functions/Present_Day compares aspects of the internet e-zine culture of the 1990s with the present day. Specifically focused on the e-zine “Integral Functions”, published in 1996, content from the zine is contrasted with recent examples of the subject matter. In what ways has the world changed and in what ways has it stayed the same?

Integral Functions

E-zine archived at textfiles.​com

1996

From issues 1 to 4:
"Integral Functions is an electronic 'zine that covers a broad range of obscure and experimental topics. Material within the magazine includes: poetry, short stories, essays, rants, reviews (books, movies and music), experimental literature (computer generated poetry, etc.), personal philosophies, chat logs, e-mail, and ASCII artwork. Topics within each issue are diverse and will change from one issue to the next."

From issues 5 onwards:
"Integral Functions is a bi-weekly electronic 'zine that encourages both stylistic and topical experimentation in all possible forms of literary self-expression."
